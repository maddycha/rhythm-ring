.Hello there! .

. This is a webring for fans of the rhythm heaven series. .

.Whether you've played the games or just appreciate the
test aesthetic & vibe, you are welcome to join!.

Requirements

  1. Must be a hand-coded personal website, social media pages & carrd will not be considered.
  2. No hate speech or nsfw content.
  3. Include the webring widget or a linkback after approval.

This webring is being maintained by: maddy

How to join

  1. Send an email at maddycha@gmail.com with the subject
    "rhythm ring application".
  2. Copy and paste this on a separate line in the email and replace uppercase text with your info.

    EXAMPLE: members.push({name: "maddy", url: "https://maddycha.com/", img: "the-dazzles-2.png"});
